Rez Wealth Planning 01 / 05
Mistake #1
No Emergency
Fund
Consequences
▶Forced into high-cost debt when life hits
▶Long-term investments get broken prematurely
▶Panic-selling during market crashes

Monthly Expense INR 50,000 → Emergency Fund = Minimum INR 3 Lakhs
Target: 6–12 months of expenses.

What To Do
✓Build 6–12 months of household expenses
✓Keep in: Liquid MF, Fixed Deposits, or High-Yield Savings
✓Never touch it unless it's a real emergency

Rez Wealth Planning 02 / 05
Mistake #2
No Health
Insurance
Consequences
▶Lifetime savings wiped in a single hospitalisation
▶Forced to sell gold & property at distressed prices
▶Entire family buried in debt from multiple sources

Your employer cover stops the day your job stops.
You need a personal policy independent of your employment.

What To Do
✓Get health insurance the moment you start earning
✓Choose: Claim Settlement Ratio 95%+, No Room Rent Cap, No Co-Pay
✓Minimum cover: INR 10 Lakhs per person

Rez Wealth Planning 03 / 05
Mistake #3
No Term
Insurance
Consequences
▶Family breaks financially when primary income vanishes
▶Outstanding loans turn into unbearable burden
▶Children's & spouse's future goals collapse entirely

Cover = 10–15x your annual income.
Premiums are cheapest when you're young & healthy. Buy now.

What To Do
✓Buy term insurance as soon as you start working
✓Cover = minimum 10–15x of annual income
✓Claim Settlement Ratio >95% · Pure term only, never mixed

Rez Wealth Planning 04 / 05
Mistake #4
Delaying
Investing
Consequences
▶Inflation erodes purchasing power every single year
▶Home, education, marriage goals become unaffordable
▶Emergencies with no corpus force massive debt

Starting at 25 vs 35 — same SIP, same amount.
The early starter retires with 3x more. Delaying 10 years costs crores.

What To Do
✓Invest minimum 20% of monthly income — non-negotiable
✓Step-up SIP by 7–10% every year as income grows
✓Never stop SIP in downturns — wealth is built there

Mistake #5
No Financial
Emergency
Folder
Consequences
▶Unclaimed wealth rots — family never sees it
▶Insurance claims lapse because no one knew the policy existed
▶Property & assets lost to legal disputes

One organised folder — physical + digital.
Takes 2 hours. Saves your family years of pain.

What To Maintain
✓Family IDs · Insurance policies · Investment folios
✓Bank accounts · Loans · Property deeds · Passwords
✓Tell one trusted family member where it is
